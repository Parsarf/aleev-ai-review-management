How it works

Four steps to better reviews

From connection to published reply in minutes — with you in control at every step.

1

Connect your profile

Sign in with your Google account and authorize ALEEV to access your Google Business Profile locations securely via OAuth.

2

View your reviews

All your customer reviews appear in one centralized inbox, sorted by urgency — flagged, needs reply, and already handled.

3

Generate AI replies

Select a review and let AI draft a response that matches your tone, brand voice, and business rules — in seconds.

4

Approve and publish

Review the draft, edit if needed, then approve. ALEEV posts the reply directly to Google — you stay in full control.

Features

Everything you need to manage reviews

Purpose-built for business owners who care about their online reputation.

✦

AI-Generated Replies

OpenAI-powered responses that sound like you — empathetic for negative reviews, warm and inviting for positive ones. Customizable tone and brand rules.

✓

Human Approval First

Nothing goes live without your say-so. Every AI draft is presented for your review and edit before posting to Google. You're always in control.

⊟

Centralized Inbox

All reviews from all your Google Business Profile locations in one place. Filter by rating, status, or location to prioritize your work.

⚡

Time-Saving Automation

Auto-reply to 5-star reviews, get crisis alerts for negative content, and track response-time metrics to continuously improve your coverage.

◉

Crisis Detection

Automatically flags reviews with serious keywords like "health department" or "unsafe" so you can respond immediately to protect your reputation.

⊕

Analytics Dashboard

Track your response coverage, average reply time, star rating trends, and sentiment distribution over time — all in one clean dashboard.
